Info: So what exactly is global warming?

Global warming is the stage in which the temperature of the earth is rapidly increasing and the atmosphere's layers that protect us from the sun’s radiation is becoming thinner and thinner each time.The planet is warming up each year due to carbon dioxide.


Effects of Global Warming:

Melting of Glaciers:

  • Because of global warming, the sea levels will rise due to the melting of the glaciers. Soon many animals such as polar bears, seals and penguins living on earth will be at risk.

  • With glaciers melting at a rapid speed soon there won’t be any way to reverse the damage that is being caused. It will put in risk ecosystems.


    • Climate Increase:

  • Increase of precipitation will occur on the coast

  • More evaporation which will cause more rain in some parts of the world

  • Animals and plants will have trouble adapting to the new climate since there will be an increase in rain.

  • Many animals and plants will not survive

  • Animals will try to migrate


    • Diseases:

  • As temperature increases it can affect the health of humans

  • Water born diseases are likely to spread like malaria


    • Rise of sea levels:

  • The melting of glaciers are causing the sea levels in the artic to rise

  • Cities near the U.S east coast are starting to have a flood damage
